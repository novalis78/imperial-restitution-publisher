About This Work

What happens when love refuses to die?

This beautiful collection of narrative poetry follows one soul's journey through devastating loss to profound understanding. Twenty-four interconnected cantos trace the path from raw grief to the recognition that true love transcends death itself.

Written in classical verse that echoes the great tradition while speaking to contemporary hearts. Perfect for anyone who has loved deeply, lost completely, or wondered about the bonds that death cannot break.
