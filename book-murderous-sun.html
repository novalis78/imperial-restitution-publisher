About This Work

A dying star. A desperate civilization. A final stand against the inevitable.

In the shadow of a sun gone mad, humanity's descendants face extinction. The Murderous Sun—once a giver of life—now threatens to consume everything in its death throes.

A sweeping space opera that spans generations and star systems, this epic tale explores what civilizations do when faced with cosmic doom. Do they surrender to fate? Fight against physics itself? Or transcend everything they once were?

In the tradition of Asimov's Foundation and Herbert's Dune, this is science fiction on a civilizational scale.
