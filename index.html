Our catalog spans classical scholarship, strategic thought, consciousness exploration, speculative fiction, and works that illuminate the hidden forces shaping our world. What unites these works is their ambition: to grapple with questions that endure.

The Publisher

Lennart Lopin

Founder & Editor-in-Chief

Born near Peenemünde, Germany, Lennart Lopin's family escaped East Germany in 1984—an experience that would forever shape his understanding of freedom, power, and the fragility of civilization.

With a Master's degree in Computer Science specializing in AI and Computational Linguistics, Lopin bridges the ancient and the cutting-edge. His thesis led to "Prelude," a championship-winning chatbot created in 2005—years before the current AI revolution.

A polyglot fluent in Latin, Pali, and Sanskrit, he has authored over 30 books spanning classical scholarship, blockchain technology, philosophy, and speculative fiction. As CTO and co-founder of Byte Federal, he continues to build at the frontier of technology while ensuring the wisdom of the past is not forgotten.
