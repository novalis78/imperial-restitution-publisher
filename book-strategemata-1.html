About This Work

Transform Your Latin Learning Through Ancient Roman Strategy.

Discover how Roman commanders outsmarted their enemies—while mastering Latin naturally through compelling historical narratives. Why struggle with grammar drills when you can absorb Latin through thrilling military tales?

Strategemata revolutionizes Latin learning by adapting Sextus Julius Frontinus' classic military strategies into a carefully structured comprehensible input experience. Instead of memorizing conjugations, you'll follow Cato as he tricks enemy cities, watch Hannibal's brilliant deceptions, and witness Scipio's tactical genius—all while naturally acquiring Latin vocabulary and grammar.

"The Hans Ørberg method meets the art of war. This is how Latin should be learned."

— Classical Studies Review
