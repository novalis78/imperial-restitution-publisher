About This Work

Elevate Your Latin Through Spectacular Military Operations.

Ready to move beyond basic Latin? Volume II elevates your learning through spectacular military operations that shaped the Roman Empire. Building on Volume I, Strategemata II presents 20 advanced chapters featuring history's most celebrated strategic masterpieces.

Follow Gylippus deceiving enemy fleets, Hanno escaping through flames, and Spartacus transforming from gladiator to strategic genius—while naturally absorbing near-classical Latin.
