About This Work

"Orwell's 1984 was a warning. This book shows it was a prediction."

What if you could witness the EXACT moment America became Oceania? You're about to discover the story they don't want you to read.

Eric Smith was just a factory worker trying to provide for his family. He believed in America, trusted the system, played by the rules. Then he watched a "free and fair" election get stolen in broad daylight—and realized the Constitution had become toilet paper.

Sound familiar?

"The past was erased, the erasure was forgotten, the lie became the truth."

— George Orwell

What happens when "temporary" emergency powers become permanent?

  • ☑ Political opponents labeled "domestic terrorists"
  • ☑ Media becomes the Ministry of Truth
  • ☑ Children encouraged to report "thoughtcrime"
  • ☑ History rewritten in real-time

This prequel explores the origins of totalitarianism, depicting how a fragmented society gradually surrenders freedom through media control, surveillance, and propaganda. The narrative follows one man's struggle to resist tyranny and protect truth for future generations.

Key Themes

  • III Language manipulation (pre-Newspeak)
